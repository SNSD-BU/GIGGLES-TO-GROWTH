Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂) Reference Values

  • SpO₂ is lower in immediate newborns.
  • Beyond newborns, values below 90–92% may indicate a respiratory issue or cyanotic heart disease.

Source: PALS Guidelines (2015), CPS Position Statement (2015)
